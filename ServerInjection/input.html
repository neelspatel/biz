Product Description

Size: 25 Strips

Human chorionic gonadotropin (hCG) is a hormone, produced by the developing placenta shortly after the conception and secreted into the urine. The pregnancy test contains antibodies which specifically react with this hormone. Negative (not pregnant): Only one color band appears, in the Control Zone. No apparent band on the Test Zone. This indicates that no pregnancy has been detected. Positive (pregnant): Distinct color bands appear in the Control and Test Zones. It indicates that you are pregnant. The color intensity of the test bands may vary since different stages of pregnancy have different concentrations of HCG hormone.

Each strip is individually wrapped.

4.0 out of 5 stars Works but line is fainter and takes longer to appear than on a drugstore test July 24, 2010
By nr danilow
Size Name:25 Strips|Amazon Verified Purchase
These strips can detect pregnancy before a missed period but potential buyers should be aware that the line takes longer to appear and is much fainter than the line you will get with one of the packaged drugstore brands. In fact, I took this test before my missed period and thought that the results were "inconclusive" because the line was barely visible so my husband went out to get a drugstore test. That test was much more obvious and results appeared much quicker. Even now, about a week plus after my period, the line is still fainter than the control line and takes a couple of minutes to appear. Hopefully that doesn't indicate a problem. Anyway, the bottom line is that if you plan on taking a lot of pregnancy tests, these are a good option just don't expect them to work the same as the other tests you may be used to taking.

UPDATE: It turned out that the pregnancy for which I originally reviewed these strips miscarried at 6.5 weeks :( This is perhaps why the line was so faint and took so long to appear. I used these again when I became pregnant in January 2011 and the line was strong and appeared quickly. Now I am 23 weeks pregnant.
